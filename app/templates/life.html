Arbors of Harbortown

Experience the excitement of Atlanta’s Midtown West neighborhood. Call Gables 820 West home and enjoy nearby King Plow Arts Center, Atlantic Station and all west side has to offer. Just moments from Downtown and Buckhead, Gables 820 West has some of the most unique amenities in town including a saline pool with aqua lounge, outdoor theater, fitness center and billiards room. You’ll also know you’re doing your part for the environment while living in responsible green luxury in one of Atlanta’s first EarthCraft-certified apartment communities. With one and two bedroom apartments offering skyline views, hardwood floors, 9’ ceilings, granite counters with brick backsplashes and unique built-in desks and bookcases, you’ll be the envy of the neighborhood.

Community Amenities

  • Club quality fitness center
  • Earthcraft and Energy Star certified
  • Gated parking and street access
  • Mezzanine coffee bar and internet lounge
  • Rentable storage space
  • Skyline views
  • Two landscaped courtyards with bbqs

Pet Policy

We are happy to accept dogs and cats. A maximum of two pets, up to 75 pounds per pet. Pet stations are located throughout the community. Additional pet fee is required. Breed restrictions do apply.
